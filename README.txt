Title: Schedule generator for teachers

Implementation Details:
Implementation Details:
1. Download android studios from this link: https://developer.android.com/studio.
2. After downloading the application, open the project after opening Android.
3. When opened, click on a dropdown menu on the top left. The default should be Project, but click on the menu, and choose android.
4. After doing this, there should be a “run” green button somewhere on the middle top right. Click on that and a simulator will run.
